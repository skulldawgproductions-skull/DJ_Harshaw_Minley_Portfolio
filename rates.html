How It Works

A simple path from first message to final files.

Step 1

Talk it through

A short call or message to understand your goals, audience, and scope.

Step 2

Quote & deposit

You get a fixed quote. A 50% deposit books the work and start date.

Step 3

Concepts

I share initial directions for your feedback, then refine over two revision rounds.

Step 4

Final files

You receive polished, ready-to-use files in every format you need.

Good to Know

  • A 50% deposit is due before work begins; the balance is due before final files are delivered.
  • Each package includes two rounds of revisions. Extra rounds are billed hourly.
  • Final files are delivered in the formats your project needs (print and web).
  • Rush projects (under one week) add a 30–50% fee.
  • Prices are starting ranges; complex scope or extended usage rights may adjust the quote.
